# Competitor analysis

Reviewed: 2026-07-15

Source: the public project gallery for the [OKX.AI Genesis Hackathon](https://www.hackquest.io/zh-cn/hackathons/OKXAI-Genesis-Hackathon). The gallery contained 17 entries at review time; directions may change before judging.

## Market map

| Direction | Visible entries | Implication |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Trading, routing, portfolio, escrow, or liquidity | StableGuard, Sentinel, StableRoute, docket arbiter, ExitGuard | Crowded and often dependent on chain or market data. |
| Transaction or agent security | SafeIntent LoopGuard, Pre-Flight Safety Scanner, Guardian | Strong direct competition around pre-execution safety. |
| General software utility | VentureOS, SubmitGuard, customer discovery agent | Useful products, but another broad assistant would be hard to distinguish. |
| Agent infrastructure | Cortex, xbird, LICENSE402, GovCoPilot | Technically strong and infrastructure-heavy. |
| Lifestyle | Falci Hatun | Only clearly visible Lifestyle project; entertainment focused. |

Low-relevance test entries were not treated as competitive signals.

## Direction decision

The hackathon awards three Lifestyle Companion prizes. A practical pet-supply decision tool occupies a repeated consumer workflow and is materially different from astrology or tarot. It also avoids competing head-on with the dense finance and transaction-security field.

Alternatives considered:

- Founder reality checks overlapped VentureOS and the customer-discovery entry.
- Transaction safety overlapped at least three visible firewall/scanner projects.
- Pet-product fit offered a narrower, demonstrable, deterministic service with no wallet funding dependency.

## PawSift differentiation

- Structured input and output suitable for agents, not free-form lifestyle chat.
- Stable authored rule IDs and reproducible SHA-256 receipts.
- One public engine shared by the web demo, examples, tests, and A2MCP endpoint.
- Seller-oriented missing-fact questions and listing patches, not only a consumer verdict.
- Explicit non-veterinary boundary and human-review routing.
- Free launch mode with no simulated payment, sales, or transaction history.

## Award strategy

Primary: Lifestyle Companion.

Secondary: Best Product and Creative Genius, supported by a polished operator console, an unusual but concrete agent use case, and proof that judges can reproduce locally or through the hosted API.
